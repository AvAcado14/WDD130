Lots of Love Site Plan

Overview

Purpose

As a college student on a budget, I wanted to make a website that could provide others with budget friendly, healthy, and time effective meals. A couple meals for breakfast, lunch, dinner, and a bonus dessert.

Audience

My intended audience would include those who are on a budget as well as college students. But it ranges to anyone who needs a time effective and healthy meal.

Wireframes

Create three wireframes for your site. One for each page and list them here

Home

The box below the navigation bar, will probably have a picture of me cooking/baking. Underneath that I would like to link in the other pages, with "Breakfast," "Lunch," and "Dinner/Dessert." Then the three meals are linked in the middle. Next I would like an image of some sort with my weekly budget friendly grocery list.

Breakfast/Lunch

The top box, below the navigation bar, will be a background, instead of a picture, with the Heading Breakfast/Lunch. There will be two breakfast recipes and two lunch recipes.

Dinner/Dessert

The top box, below the navigation bar, will be a background, instead of a picture, with the Heading Dinner/Dessert. There will be two dinner recipes and one dessert recipe.
