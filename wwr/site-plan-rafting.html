Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to provide life-changing experiences that positively impact rafters, guides, and river enthusiasts in the great outdoors. Here at Reel in Rafting, teamwork really makes the dream work! We would like you to be a part of our dream.

Audience

Reel in the rapids! We offer rafting tours for families and individuals age 6 and above. Reel in Rafting accesses accommodates a range of rivers, rapids, and rafts. Expeditions are designed with comfort, accessibility, and your experience in mind. Whether you are a first-time rafter or an expert, we welcome you to experience the beauty of Idaho's best whitewater.
