Tips

  • I like to buy in bulk, on sale, in season, frozen, and generic brands as much as possible.
  • Soups are my go to, or something that can last multiple days.
  • I like to make big portions for dinners and then use the leftovers for lunches the next few days.
  • To get a variety I buy chicken one week and beef another week, but I rotate back and forth. Same with veggies and fruits.
  • It is also about where you shop. For example a Walmart will be cheaper than an Albertsons or Broliums. A Winco would be the best place to shop.
